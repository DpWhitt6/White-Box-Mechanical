ABOUT US

Who are we?

White Box Mechanical offers a packaged solution for the design, construction and delivery of mechanical services packages tailored to suit your project requirements. Based in Gloucester, our team operates on contracts across the UK and Ireland.

What do we do?

Our ability to deliver complex projects, positions White Box Mechanical as a preferred sub-contractor with a number of leading clients, with a high level of repeat business. We are highly skilled in design and installation of mechanical services on all types of commercial projects including retail, leisure, warehouses, office spaces, the public sector etc.

Why choose us?

White Box Mechanical take pride in continuous professional development and investment to deliver the best results to our clients. We invest in achieving accreditations and certifications as required for our clients to meet their obligations to health, safety and the environment. Experienced in the mechanical and public health services sector, with a reputation for being reliable, professional, and competitive. We operate with the ethos to forge lasting relationships with clients, provide an unrivalled service with a positive attitude and employ skilled engineers with an extensive knowledge of mechanical services engineering across the spectrum.
